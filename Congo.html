The Democratic Republic of the Congo,[b] also known as DR Congo, DRC, or Congo-Kinshasa is a country in Central Africa. By land area the Congo is the second-largest country in Africa and the 11th-largest in the world.

With a population of around 109 million, the Democratic Republic of the Congo is the most populous Francophone country in the world. The national capital and largest city is Kinshasa, which is also the economic center.
